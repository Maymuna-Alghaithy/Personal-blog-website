About Me

Know more about me

I'm Maimuna Al Ghaithi

A Software developer and front-end engineer living in Seeb, Muscat, Sultanate of Oman

I'm passionate about creating and developing clean, unique, elegant products. I specialize in building Websites for small to medium size business. I keep my code clean, readable, modular and well refactored. I enjoy working with complex user interfaces that require attention to detail and a focus on usability.


In addition to website development, I also have experience in front-end frameworks such as Laravel and React Native, allowing me to create dynamic and interactive web applications. I believe in staying up-to-date with the latest industry trends and technologies, constantly expanding my skill set to deliver cutting-edge solutions. I strive to create not just functional websites, but visually appealing ones that leave a lasting impression on users. With a keen eye for design and a passion for crafting seamless user experiences, I am dedicated to bringing clients' visions to life and helping their businesses thrive online.

My inbox is always open. Whether you have a question or just want to say hi or want to hire me, I'll try my best to get back to you!
